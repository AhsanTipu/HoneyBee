About Us

These are very trying times we are living where technology is called upon
to play it’s a vital part of helping us overcome this world-wide crisis.

During the COVID-19 crisis thousands of businesses were affected and
millions of people lost their jobs.

We are launching the "Honey Bee Jobs" web portal a one-stop-shop to bridge the gap between the businesses with vacancies and the job seekers globally so that businesses can easily recruit amazing local candidates and save tons of money on recruitment, advertisements and job placements.

The name "Honey Bee Jobs" has been carefully chosen to recognize the efforts of a tiny honey bee, who works tirelessly collecting nectar from the flowers and producing honey for our consumption without any compensation only by the command of Allah.

We only ask you to please switch off your cellphones near the beehives and support your local honey farmers by consuming locally produced honey as much as you can.
